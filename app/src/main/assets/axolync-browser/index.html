How Axolync works

  • Axolync listens to real-world audio and syncs displayed lyrics. It does not play music in normal mode.
  • Start begins listening + sync flow. Stop resets everything and also stops demo audio playback.
  • Pause stops sync updates and re-adjustments. By default, lyrics keep flowing while paused (can be changed in Settings).
  • Resume runs a short re-sync phase and continues timeline display.
  • Drag in the lyrics area to change context lines: drag down shows fewer lines (zoom in), drag up shows more lines (zoom out), double-click resets to 5 lines.
  • Demo mode is a special case where sample audio can play for presentation.
